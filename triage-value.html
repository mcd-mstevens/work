Do users who click the triage agent engage more with alerts? Three lenses on the same question, each controlling for more confounders than the last.

The correlation survives three increasingly strict controls. Triage users engage more, that lift holds when comparing the same people before and after their first triage click, and it shows up on the specific days they click triage vs days they don’t. All three lenses point the same direction.

Remaining confounder: users may fire up triage on days with more alerts, and more alerts = more engagement regardless of triage. A randomized A/B test (show/hide the button) would close that gap.

Gap
The triage agent’s recommended priority output (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW) is not being stored in Snowflake or logged as a Mixpanel event property. The “Priority popover opened” event fires but carries no payload. To enable a priority-level breakdown of downstream engagement, add triage_priority as a property on the In-Product Triage Mixpanel event.
